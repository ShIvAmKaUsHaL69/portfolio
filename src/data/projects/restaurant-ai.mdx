# Restaurant Customer Retention AI

## Overview

An AI-powered system designed for restaurants and food shop owners to retain customers through personalized image messages. Send your customers a beautiful WhatsApp message with their order details and a friendly reminder to visit again.

## Features

- **Personalized Messages** — Custom WhatsApp messages with order details for each customer
- **Image Generation** — Beautiful, branded image messages generated automatically
- **Self-Hosted** — Everything runs on your own server, no extra costs
- **Customer Loyalty** — Friendly reminders that drive repeat visits
- **Zero Third-Party Cost** — Completely self-hosted solution
