# Lead Scraper

## Overview

Lead Scraper is an automated tool that fetches leads from Google Maps, cleans data, detects emails, and exports structured datasets. Simply enter what you're looking for (like "restaurants in Chandigarh"), choose how many leads you want, add your email, and receive a detailed leads list right in your inbox.

## Features

- **Google Maps Scraping** – Automatically fetches business data from Google Maps based on search queries
- **Data Cleaning** – Processes and cleans raw data for accuracy
- **Email Detection** – Identifies and extracts email addresses from business listings
- **Structured Export** – Exports clean, structured datasets ready for use
- **Email Delivery** – Sends the leads list directly to your inbox

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Scraping**: Puppeteer (headless Chrome)
